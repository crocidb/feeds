Dave the Diver is a delight. It is also a:

Resource gathering game  
Restaurant sim  
Rhythm game  
Bullet-hell shmup  
Mini-game collection (think Warioware, Iron Chef-style)  
Farming sim

It is all those things while also secretly advocating that we take care of our greatest natural resource, our oceans.

Don’t waste your time reading any more reviews for Dave the Diver. Just [go get it](https://store.steampowered.com/app/1868140/DAVE_THE_DIVER/) and play. I promise you will finish the first day with a smile on your face.

9.5/10

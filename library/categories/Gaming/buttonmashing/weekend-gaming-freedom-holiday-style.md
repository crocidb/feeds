It has been wall-to-wall [Legend of Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom](https://buttonmashing.com/2023/05/12/in-my-weekend-gaming-hands-15/) around the Casa de Buttonmashing since release, but now I’ve been feeling like it’s time for a little break. Not because TotK is getting boring, definitely not, it’s just time for something different. A palette cleanser, perhaps. Or maybe I just want to shoot something with a weapon other than a bow. Either way, the closed beta for [The Division: Heartland](https://www.ubisoft.com/en-us/game/the-division/heartland) came across the inbox this week so I downloaded it and decided to give it a whirl over the long weekend. What better way to celebrate freedom than by shooting something in the face!?

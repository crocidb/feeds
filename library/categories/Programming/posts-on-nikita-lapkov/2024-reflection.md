Holy shit, what a year that was. It was absolutely bonkers overwhelming. A lot of interesting stuff happened, but at the same time I took on so much more than I could handle.

Conferences
----------

![Photo of me speaking at RustLab](https://laplab.me/posts/2024-reflection/rustlab-speaking.webp)

>
>
> Speaking at RustLab 2024
>
>

During late 2023 and early 2024 I applied to a bunch of conferences. Previously, I didn’t have any luck of becoming a speaker at non-Russian conferences - all of my talk proposals were rejected (with a notable exception of Handmade Seattle, which happened late 2023). I was expecting at most one conference reaching out to me and inviting me to speak. Instead, I was very humbled (and overwhelmed!) to get accepted to **five** conferences this year. This was absolutely unexpected and I am very grateful to the organisers for trusting me on delivering a good talk. Due to logistical and financial reasons, I was only able to accept invitations to four conferences - which was still 4x of my best expectations.

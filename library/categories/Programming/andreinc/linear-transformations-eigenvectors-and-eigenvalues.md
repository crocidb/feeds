Introduction
==========

There are rumors saying Computer Vision Engineers consider **Eigenvalues** and **Eigenvectors** the single most important concept(s) in linear algebra. I am not 100% sure about that, but I must admit those two things confused me a lot during my university years.

I think my teacher lost me somewhere in between *The basis of a vector space* and *Linear transformations*. N-dimensional vectors rotating?! That was too much for me. So I stopped caring, learned the formulas, and passed the exam. Here I am more than ten years later, interested in A.I. algorithms and realizing those two notions are much simpler than I thought.

FAQ
==========

<details open=""> <summary>**What was your first programming language and computer?**</summary>

My first language was BASIC on an [HC91](https://ro.wikipedia.org/wiki/HC_91), a Romanian microcomputer based on the Z80. My parents bought me one in 93.

</details> <details open=""> <summary>**What was the first program you ever wrote?**</summary>

The first thing I ever "coded" was a joint effort with my dad, who was actually trying to learn how to program at that time. We sat down together and managed to draw a bicycle on that [HC91](https://ro.wikipedia.org/wiki/HC_91). A total breakthrough.

</details>

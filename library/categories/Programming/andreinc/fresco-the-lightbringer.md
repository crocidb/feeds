Some jokes write themselves. In our case, the electrician wrote it.

Let me give you a little context.

Summers in Bucharest can get brutally hot, and when it gets that hot, your brain simply stops functioning. So on weekends, I prefer the cool breeze of the lakes surrounding our capital city.

[Bucharest](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bucharest) was actually built on a swampy area, so lakes are practically everywhere. One of the most amazing places to escape to just outside the city is the [Cernica Monastery](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cernica_Monastery). Established in 1608, it sits peacefully on two islands right in the middle of Lake Cernica.

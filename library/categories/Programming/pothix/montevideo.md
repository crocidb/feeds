In March 2019 happened the Rust LATAM 2019, which I was one of the main organizers. It happened in Montevideo, so I had some time to also walk around the city and get to know a few places. My plan was to visit a few more places, but the Doist retreat would happen the day after the conference and I wouldn’t have enough time to do anything before. would recommend people to stop there, see the train station and move on.

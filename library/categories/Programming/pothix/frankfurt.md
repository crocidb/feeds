Frankfurt was just a connecting city for me. I was going to Bad Liebenzell and LATAM has direct flights from São Paulo to Frankfurt. Airport The airport is good and I was able to go through security quite quickly. Many people change planes in Frankfurt and I had the chance to be in the first seats, so I left before other people. These two facts helped me to go straight to the border control and go through the process quite fast.

In June 2022 I had the chance to spend 3 days in Munich while preparing for the Doist retreat. Unfortunately, I was not feeling well in the second day of the trip, so it was mostly 2 working days, but I enjoyed it. It was interesting to see how Germans are totally fine on doing topless or changing in public places. Not in the street, but close to the river, for example.

I had the chance to spend 10 hours in Miami in a long layover in September 2023, and will describe my experience here. Airport Nothing to complain about the airport, it worked well for me. The location is not that bad, and I could get an Uber to places I wanted to visit in the city. I got there in \~20 minutes. VIP Lounge I was able to try the Turkish Airlines lounge and it was an OK-ish experience.

Book: The Silmarilion by J.R.R. Tolkien. Rating: ⭐️⭐️⭐️⭐️⭐️. This is J.R.R. Tolkien’s life work. The other known books a product of this. He was not able to finish this masterpiece, but Christopher Tolkien finished his father’s work in a great way. I’m not going to lie, the Silmarilion is not an easy to read book. I would not recommend it to anyone who was not totally aware of the other books and still looking for more middle earth content.

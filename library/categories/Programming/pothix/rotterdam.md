In 2017 I had the chance to speak at FOSDEM in Brussels, and I used this opportunity to do a bigger travel through a few countries in Europe. After visiting a few cities in Belgium, I went up to Rotterdam. Stay I stayed in the cube houses, which is a very iconic place in Rotterdam and it’s now a hostel! It was an amazing experience and I totally recommend it.

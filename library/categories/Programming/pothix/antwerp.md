In 2017 I had the chance to speak at FOSDEM in Brussels, and I used this opportunity to travel around Belgium a bit, one city every day. One of the cities I visited was Antwerp. Being very honest, it’s definitely not a city I would visit again. The best part of the city for me was the train station, which is trully beautiful. I would recommend people to stop there, see the train station and move on.

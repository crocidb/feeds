In July 2023, the Doist retreat happened in Artimino, which is part of the province of Prato. My descriptions here will be only related to that part of Prato. Stay I stayed in the Artimino village, on the other side of the Artimino hotel, which is close to the historic house of the Medici. It’s a medieval city and contains many different rooms of this hotel (\~90 rooms) and a few restaurants.

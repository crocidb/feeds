Hey everyone!

This is the list of all the changes we've done to our projects during the month of March.

![little ninj, a plus ninja, is examining at the contents of a first-aid kit](../media/content/videos/march_2025.jpg)

Summary Of Changes
----------

In the above illustration, little Ninj is going through a [first-aid kit](https://rabbitwaves.ca/site/first_aid_kit.html), looking through our supplies to see what needs to be topped off and what is out-of-date. Rek drew a list of suggestions on what to include in both a first-aid and a medical kit for the Rabbit Waves project, we plan to add more items soon(thanks to everyone on Mastodon who suggested additions! It'll be in the April update).

We will spend the first few days of April participating in [Flickjam](https://itch.io/jam/flickjam-2025), making small games in the style of [Flickgame](https://www.flickgame.org/index.html), a tool originally made by Increpare, in which the world is navigated by clicking on pixels of different colors to head in different directions. Devine ported [Flickgame to Varvara](https://wiki.xxiivv.com/site/flick.html), and wrote a compiler for flick games to uxn roms.

This past month, Rek finished transcribing the entire 15 weeks of the [Victoria to Sitka logbook](../site/victoria_to_sitka_logbook.html)! We have plans to turn it into a book, in the style of [Busy Doing Nothing](../site/busy_doing_nothing.html), with tons of extra content and illustrations.

March was a very good month for silly [calendar doodles](https://rabbits.srht.site/days/2025/03/31.html). Our paper calendar is always in view, it documents important events like releases, appointments, as well as food, memes, and other noteworthy things that happened on each day.

**Book Club:** This month we are still reading *The Goldfinch* by Donna Tartt(it's a long book).

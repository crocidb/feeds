Hey everyone!

This is the list of all the changes we've done to our projects during the month of April.

![](https://100r.ca/media/content/videos/april_2024.jpg)

Summary Of Changes
----------

* **100r.ca**, added [Twilight](https://100r.ca/site/twilight.html), updated our notes on [chainplates](https://100r.ca/site/chainplates.html#april2024), [wheel to tiller conversion](https://100r.ca/site/wheel_to_tiller_conversion.html#apr2024), [cabin lights](https://100r.ca/site/dc_electrical_refit.html#apr2024) [standing rigging replacement](https://100r.ca/site/standing_rigging_replacement.html#apr2024), [Princess Louisa Inlet](https://100r.ca/site/princess_louisa_inlet.html)(added maps), [Goji no Chaimu](https://100r.ca/site/goji_no_chaimu.html)(added maps), and [Sailing to Japan](https://100r.ca/site/sailing_in_japan.html)(fixed some dead links).
* **[Hakum](https://kokorobot.ca/site/hakum.html)**, released a new comic sequence called [Kaizah](https://kokorobot.ca/site/kaizah.html).
* **[Wunderland Rabbits](https://kokorobot.ca/site/wunderland_rabbits.html)**, released a new [Rabbit travel photo](https://kokorobot.ca/media/content/wunderland_13_full.jpg)
* **[Orca](https://100r.ca/site/orca.html)**, added a note on the repository for the Javascript version titled [Is Orca dead?](https://github.com/hundredrabbits/Orca/issues/297)

Devine has been busy working on the [implementation](https://git.sr.ht/~rabbits/modal) and [documentation](https://wiki.xxiivv.com/site/modal) of [wryl](https://wryl.tech/)'s fantastic programming language *Modal*. Rewriting systems are a [computation paradigm](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Abstract_rewriting_machine) that is generally unknown and under-explored that might have some fascinating features that might be able help us to tackle some of our future projects.

In other news, Pino is ready to head northward! We finished all of our boats projects and left the dock on May 1st. See a [photo](https://100r.ca/media/content/travel/se_ak_01.jpg) of our first day of the year on the water, taken as we exited Enterprise Channel, just north of Trial Island south of Victoria. On our travels we will continue to push updates every month like usual, but the updates will only go live when we find internet, and this may or may not coincide with the start of every month. We will keep a log of our travels, populated with plenty of drawings!

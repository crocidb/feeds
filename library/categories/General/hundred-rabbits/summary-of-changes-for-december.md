Hey everyone! This is the list of all the changes we've done to our projects and apps during the month of December. We'll also be reporting in our on position in the world, and on our future plans.

Summary Of Changes
----------

* **100r.ca**, added a new page: [electrical refit](https://100r.ca/site/electrical_refit.html), [fuel sensor](https://100r.ca/site/fuel_sensor.html), [marine wire termination](https://100r.ca/site/marine_wire_termination.html), also added updates to XPS and EPS in [winter insulation](https://100r.ca/site/winter_insulation.html).
* **[Orca](https://100r.ca/site/orca.html)**, released a new build that improves the look and feel, added [app icon](https://merveilles.town/@neauoire/109498859357833815)(Mastodon) to the about modal.
* **[Busy Doing Nothing](https://100r.ca/site/busy_doing_nothing.html)**, released a new version, in which we fixed extra typing errors (thanks Igor), and added explanations for certain sailing terms like reefing, etc.
* **[Wiktopher](https://100r.ca/site/wiktopher.html)**, corrected chapter 6 and 7.
* [Beetbug](http://wiki.xxiivv.com/site/beetbug.html), added break-point and [symbols](http://wiki.xxiivv.com/site/symbols.html) supports.
* Took a detour into computer science to learn about [arity testing and stack effect](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=wIx1aCiRZl8) checking, learn more about it [here](https://docs.factorcode.org/content/article-inference.html).
* TinyCode2022, spent some time doing [demoscene experiments](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=V1eQV_DKRl0).

### News ###

The rabbits are re-wiring Pino. So far this month, we spent entire afternoons removing, and passing new wires into the boat's walls and ceiling. This project has taken up most of our time this month. For updates on the project, see [electrical refit](https://100r.ca/site/electrical_refit.html). We also released an edited transcript of Devine's talk [weathering software winter](https://100r.ca/site/weathering_software_winter.html), for those who prefer to read.

We forgot to mention it in the last update, but Compudanzas just released a new version of their [introduction to uxn programming book](https://compudanzas.itch.io/introduction-to-uxn-programming/devlog/451234/version-104-launcher-and-raw-runes)! The [online tutorial](https://compudanzas.net/uxn_tutorial.html) also had some updates.

Happy new year everyone! See more photos of [Pino](https://100r.ca/media/content/stream/2022_gvha02.jpg)(on the far left) and [friends](https://100r.ca/media/content/stream/2022_gvha01.jpg) decorated for the occasion.

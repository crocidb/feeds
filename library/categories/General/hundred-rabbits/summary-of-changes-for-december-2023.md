Hey everyone!

This is the list of all the changes we've done to our projects during the month of December.

Summary Of Changes
----------

* **100r.ca**, added [solar cooking experiment](https://100r.ca/site/solar_cooking_experiment.html), [making seamed box cushions](https://100r.ca/site/making_seamed_box_cushions.html), [making saloon cushions](https://100r.ca/site/making_saloon_cushions.html), [moisture prevention underliner](https://100r.ca/site/moisture_prevention_underliner.html), and [board games](https://100r.ca/site/board_games.html). Updated [charging electronics](https://100r.ca/site/charging_electronics.html#dec2023), and [upholstery](https://100r.ca/site/upholstery.html).
* **[Wiktopher](https://100r.ca/site/wiktopher.html)**, released the book on [paperback](https://www.lulu.com/shop/rek-bell-and-devine-lu-linvega/wiktopher/paperback/product-m22wv96.html?page=1&pageSize=4).
* **[Wunderland Rabbits](https://kokorobot.ca/site/wunderland_rabbits.html)**, added a new [photo](https://kokorobot.ca/media/content/wunderland_12.jpg).
* **[GrimGrains](http://grimgrains.com)**, added [millet dumplings](http://grimgrains.com/site/millet_dumplings.html), and [vegetable curry](http://grimgrains.com/site/vegetable_curry.html).
* **[December Adventure](https://rabbits.srht.site/decadv/)**, Devine wrote a daily devlog for the month, documenting various projects like Porporo(an OS).
* **[Lispkit](https://git.sr.ht/~rabbits/lispkit)**, wrote a [PureLisp](http://wiki.xxiivv.com/site/lisp.html) compiler and emulator for the [SECD stack-machine](http://wiki.xxiivv.com/site/secd.html).
* **[Grail](http://wiki.xxiivv.com/site/grail.html)**, made a little shorthand calligraphy and interpreter.

### News ###

Wiktopher was released on paperback! Our small collection of [self-published books](https://100r.ca/media/content/inventory/wik_bdn.jpg) is growing.

We finished the upholstery in the saloon, Pino feels like a new boat. We also published an article on this past summer's [Solar Cooking Experiment](https://100r.ca/site/solar_cooking_experiment.html).

Last September Devine and a group of people went on a 3-day train ride from Seattle to St Louis for the last edition of [Strange Loop 2023](https://thestrangeloop.com/). This [video](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9qVT0URkkGE) documents that journey.

As mentionned in November's update, [Tinyletter](https://tinyletter.com/hundredrabbits), the service we use to send out our monthly newsletter, is **shutting down** in February 2024. We will now be using Sourcehut to send our monthly updates. With this new system our emails will be leaner than ever, using plain text(no html). We cannot transfer accounts to this new list ourselves, so **if you want to keep receiving updates by email please [sign up again here](https://lists.sr.ht/~rabbits/newsletter)**. Clicking on the subscribe button will open your email client, you can leave the body and subject of the email blank. We will keep sending newsletters with TinyLetter until the end of January 2024, so as to give people time to make the switch. If you sign up to this new list, unsuscribe from the old newsletter to avoid getting two emails with the same content for December and January. We hope you continue to follow our updates.

Note that since it's our first time sending updates with the new newsletter format, it may look a little wonky. We'll improve on it next month.

2023 was kind to us, we look forward to seeing what 2024 brings! We hope the coming year treats you all well.

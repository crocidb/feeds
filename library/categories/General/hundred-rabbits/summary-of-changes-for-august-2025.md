Hey everyone!

This is the list of all the changes we've done to our projects during the month of August.

<img src="../media/content/videos/august_2025.jpg" alt="a rabbit is sitting amongst a pile of cushions, with the uxn system in one hand and the heart of the system, a beet, in the other" loading="lazy" width="1200" height="679">

* **[Wiktopher](https://100r.ca/site/wiktopher.html)**, updated [songs](https://hundredrabbits.itch.io/wiktopher-desert-tales/devlog/1006393/updated-ilken-songs).
* **100r.ca**, updated [cast iron cookware](https://100r.ca/site/cast_iron_cookware.html)(seasoning oil suggestions).
* **[Punk Rabbits](https://kokorobot.ca/site/punk_rabbits.html)**, completed 15 new rabbits(#19-34).
* **[Orca](https://100r.ca/site/orca.html)**, pushed a little update that includes the *string* operator.
* **[Solresol](https://wiki.xxiivv.com/site/solresol.html)**, illustrated some [curwen signs](https://wiki.xxiivv.com/media/generic/solresol_curwen.png).

In preparation for a programming class that's coming up, we've implemented a graphical tiny-BASIC runtime called [Sunflower BASIC](https://hundredrabbits.itch.io/sunflower-basic). It contains the essential blocks of a BASIC language interpreter with enough features to communicate elementary programming concepts and how each one is implemented. We've also released the [Permacomputing 101](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=BNYxAdjl1f0) talk given at [Critical Signals 2025](https://criticalsignals.nz/) which covered a few interesting aspect of digital preservation and some tactics to craft software in a way that may last.

Choosing to remain in the Southern Gulf islands in August this year has permitted us to pick blackberries, [plums](https://rabbits.srht.site/days/2025/08/18.html) and apples. We have incorporated the fruit into [cakes](https://grimgrains.com/site/stovetop_blackberry_cake.html), or just squished whole berries over morning toast. We processed some of the apples into [jam](https://grimgrains.com/site/chunky_apple_jam.html), and are currently delighting in eating through the rest. We've also been experimenting with the [solar cooker](https://100r.ca/site/solar_cooking.html), preparing some [cinnamon buns](https://rabbits.srht.site/days/2025/08/31.html) in it for the first time! See our cameo in our friend Peter's [latest video](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZiOcD4e7n6g).

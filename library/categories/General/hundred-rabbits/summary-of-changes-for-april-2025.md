Hey everyone!

This is the list of all the changes we've done to our projects during the month of April.

<img src="https://100r.ca/media/content/videos/april_2025.jpg" alt="little ninj, a plus ninja, is cleaning up the boat, wielding a mop " loading="lazy" width="1200" height="679">

* **100r.ca**, updated [water](https://100r.ca/site/water.html), [ditch bag](https://100r.ca/site/ditch_bag.html), [woodstove installation](https://100r.ca/site/woodstove_installation.html#apr2025), and added new photos and information on [first-aid kit](https://100r.ca/site/first_aid_kit.html).
* **[Rabbit Waves](https://100r.ca/site/rabbit_waves.html)**, updated [Triangular Bandages](https://rabbitwaves.ca/site/triangular_bandage.html) with animated gifs, and [First-Aid kit](https://rabbitwaves.ca/site/first_aid_kit.html) with new med suggestions (also appended a .txt list of meds and their intended use).
* **[Hakum](https://kokorobot.ca/site/hakum.html)**, began a new comic sequence named [Sabotage Study](https://kokorobot.ca/site/sabotage_study.html)(not yet completed).
* **[Orca](https://100r.ca/site/orca.html)**, modified the behavior of the lowercase-j operator to allow for jumpers to grow.
* **[Solresol](https://wiki.xxiivv.com/site/solresol.html)**, improved the documentation.
* **[Uxntal](https://wiki.xxiivv.com/site/uxntal.html)**, improved the documentation.

The weather is getting warmer, which is perfect for airing out Pino's lockers, and drying off moldy clothes and tools. Anything stored in the v-berth lockers, below the waterline, suffer from extreme wetness. It is a very, very annoying fact of boat life, but there is really no way to bring good air flow in those spaces. We scrubbed the lockers clean, parted with items we no longer needed, and sent two laptops to the recycler.

In last month's update, we mentioned Flickjam, a game jam based on Increpare's [Flickgame](https://www.flickgame.org/index.html). We received a total of [27 entries](https://itch.io/jam/flickjam-2025/entries)! They're really fun, and all playable in the browser. [Devine's jam entry](https://hundredrabbits.itch.io/rabbit-learns-rabbit) is about a very adorable rabbit learning to play the word "rabbit" on a xylophone in [Solresol](https://wiki.xxiivv.com/site/solresol.html).

Devine spent some time off the computer, [skating](https://wiki.xxiivv.com/site/skates.html) and [folding paper](https://wiki.xxiivv.com/site/paper_logic.html). The [paper computer](https://wiki.xxiivv.com/site/paper_computer.html) pages have been updated to cover some new ways in which computer emulators can be operated on paper. While on that subject, we highly recommend Tadashi Tokieda's excellent talk named [A world from a sheet of paper](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8p02DtmyQhU).

Another item on Devine's list was to gradually phase out [Uxnasm.c](https://wiki.xxiivv.com/etc/uxnasm.c.txt) in favor of the [self-hosted](https://wiki.xxiivv.com/site/drifblim.html) assembler. We're not 100% pleased yet, but it is getting closer to retirement.

Starting on May 20th 2025(1000 PST/PDT) the [Playdate Catalogue](https://play.date/games/) will include Oquonie. The game is also available on our [itch.io store](https://hundredrabbits.itch.io/).

The video for Devine's November 2024 talk [A Shining Place Built Upon The Sand](https://100r.ca/site/shining_sand.html) is now on [YouTube](https://youtu.be/EACF2VEpO3Q).

**Book Club:** This month we are reading *Banvard's Folly* by Paul Collins, *Einstein's Dreams* by Alan Lightman, and we are still making progress on the *The Goldfinch* by Donna Tartt.

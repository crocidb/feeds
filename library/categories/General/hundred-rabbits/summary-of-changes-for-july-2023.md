Hey everyone! This is the list of all the changes we've done to our projects and apps during the month of July. We'll also be reporting in our on position in the world, and on our future plans.

Summary Of Changes
----------

* **Slide Rule**, released a little [slide rule toy](https://merveilles.town/@neauoire/110664076683550080)(Mastodon).
* **100r.ca**, added [roscoe bay](https://100r.ca/site/roscoe_bay.html), updated [LPG refit](https://100r.ca/site/lpg_refit.html#jul2023).
* **[wiktopher](https://100r.ca/site/wiktopher.html)**, we finished doing the entire first pass of corrections.
* **[Left](https://100r.ca/site/left.html)**, made a couple of interface improvements, it's not only prettier now, but faster.
* **[Drifblim](https://git.sr.ht/~rabbits/drifblim)**, added support for lambdas in Uxntal, now also supported in [uxnasm](https://git.sr.ht/~rabbits/uxn/tree/main/item/src/uxnasm.c).

### News ###

We spent many quiet days in [Roscoe Bay](https://100r.ca/site/roscoe_bay.html), and then wandered over to [melanie cove](https://100r.ca/site/melanie_cove.html#jul2023) for a few days before moving to our favorite anchorage in Desolation Sound, [Hathayim Marine Park](https://100r.ca/site/hathayim_marine_park.html#jul2023). This inlet is quiet, and not overly busy, it is ideal for focusing on projects. We walk the 3 km trail to Squirrel Cove everyday.

We have been hard at work reviewing [Wiktopher](https://100r.ca/site/wiktopher.html), and we're happy to announce that we've finished the first pass of corrections! We'll be doing many more passes, but this was a very big step. Rek has been [drawing](https://merveilles.town/@rek/110788283194637214)(Mastodon) a lot, and Devine has been working on their presentation for [Strange Loop 2023](https://thestrangeloop.com/2023/sessions.html).

Check out this amazing [Uxn cheat sheet](https://github.com/weeble/uxn-cheatsheet) by Nettie!

### Pino book & movie club ###

We are reading Lewis Carrol's **Bruno and Sylvie**. We re-watched Vampire Hunter D for the hundredth time.

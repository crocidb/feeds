### News ###

The days are getting shorter, and so is the time that we can spend on the computer, due to our batteries depleting earlier a bit each day. So, Devine spends evenings proof-reading the [wiktopher](https://100r.ca/site/wiktopher.html) manuscript, reading and messing around with the Playdate, and Rek continues ink work on an [upcoming project](https://merveilles.town/@rek/109031721721868768)(Mastodon, no project page yet).

While cleaning up the boat, Devine found two small black notebooks. We started paging through them, to see if we should keep them. The notebooks were full of sketches, interspersed with shopping lists, and incomplete logs from earlier sailing trips we'd done. We found logs detailing our very first attempt at sailing offshore, our sail down the US West Coast and Mexico, and our passage from Tonga to New Zealand. We read the logs, and decided to transcribe and publish them online. Read the [lost logbook](https://100r.ca/site/lost_logbook.html).

Devine wrote a little [timer program](https://git.sr.ht/~rabbits/arduino-cookbook/tree/main/item/adafruit-playground/teatime.ino) for the Adafruit Playground.

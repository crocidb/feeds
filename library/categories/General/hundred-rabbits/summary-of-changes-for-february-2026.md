Hey everyone!

This is the list of all the changes we've done to our projects during the month of February.

<img src="../media/content/videos/february_2026.jpg" alt="a rabbit in a dress standing in front of a black board covered with Uxntal glyphs" loading="lazy" width="1200" height="679">

* **Wiki**, updated [knots](../site/knots.html#f8) with a figure-eight follow-through knot, also, Pino has a new [headsail](../site/headsail.html#feb2026)!
* **[Pavol](https://git.sr.ht/~rabbits/pavol)**, created a [volume control tool](../media/content/stream/pavol.jpg).
* **[Adelie](../site/adelie.html)**, made it so images are drawn faster.
* **[Turye](https://wiki.xxiivv.com/site/turye.html)**, added support for [24x24 pixels uf3 fonts](../media/content/stream/turye_24.jpg).
* **[Uxntal](https://wiki.xxiivv.com/site/uxntal.html)**, collected generic routines and commonly used objects into [example libraries](https://wiki.xxiivv.com/site/uxntal_library.html).

At the start of the month, we spent a delightful couple of days deep in the wintry and rainy [Spanish countryside](https://rabbits.srht.site/days/2026/02/11.html), gathered in a medieval monastery with the [Ink & Switch](https://www.inkandswitch.com/) team for the 10 years anniversary of the studio. We also met up with Javier and Marcelino who gave us coffee and a book on [celestial navigation](https://rabbits.srht.site/days/2026/03/01.html), we will savor a cup while studying the sky.

We asked folks on the fediverse for their favourite rabbits from Rek's [recent series](https://kokorobot.ca/site/punk_rabbits.html), and picked out the four most popular and made [stickers](../site/punkrabbits_stickers.html). We received them just yesterday and they look amazing! Thanks to everyone who already asked for a sheet! We pack the letters by hand, and Dev makes a [spirograph](../media/content/stream/letters_date.jpg) on the back of each one encoding the date in gear radius and ratio.

We've hit some annoying issues with Arch, where mainstay programs sudden change their behavior or gets abandoned like *volumeicon*. Being unable to control the master volume without opening a full GUI application is a pain. Devine [to the rescue](../media/content/stream/pavol.jpg)! An hour's work and [Pavol](https://git.sr.ht/~rabbits/pavol) was born! In that same vein, while working on the new version of [Donsol](../site/donsol.html), we realized we'd need to design fonts larger than 16 px high, something that [Turye](https://wiki.xxiivv.com/site/turye.html) was incapable of doing, until now.

On March 6th, we'll be giving a [talk](https://kulaacademy.ca/rabbits/) at the University Of Victoria, to talk about how our 10 years of living on the water has influenced our design and development work. The event is sold out at this point, but if you signed up see you there! [Oquonie](../site/oquonie.html) for Playdate will be on Playdate's upcoming Catalog Anniversary Sale running from March 5th to March 19th.

Devine has been puzzling over an [extremely odd programming language idea](https://wiki.xxiivv.com/site/bagel.html) and is looking for some pointers, prior art, ideas, so if ferns, rewriting, confluence, linear logic, tensor and multisets mean anything to you and interests you, get in touch. Lastly, we'll leave you with this [image of Polycat](https://rabbits.srht.site/days/2026/02/28.html) working on a 2009 EeePC under HaikuOS, courtesy of [Tbsp](https://merveilles.town/@tbsp)!

**Book Club:** We finally finished reading *Middlemarch* by George Eliot, a very good read, we will miss it. We are also reading *Red Plenty* by Francis Spufford and *This Is How You Lose the Time War* by Amal El-Mohtar and Max Gladstone. We also watched great movies like The Battle of Algiers(1966) and Z(1969).

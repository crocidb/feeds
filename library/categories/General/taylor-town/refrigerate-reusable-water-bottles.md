1. Purchase [glass bottles](https://www.amazon.com/Juice-Bottles-Smoothies-Stainless-Borosilicate/dp/B07P91TZNS).
2. Fill the bottles with water.
3. Refrigerate the bottles.
4. Enjoy chilled water at your leisure.

---

Benefits of chilled water bottles:

* Never live life on the edge! Cups lack clever caps! Lids kill spills!
* Ice clumps in cups and falls on your face.
* Commence your commute with a chilled treat on the street! Bottles are portable!
* Fancy glass bottles impress guests.
* Ice wastes valuable volume and dilutes drinks.
* Disposable water bottles cost money! And reclying is annoying!
* Ice is creepy.
* Convenient cold water offers an appealing alternative to bad beverages and detrimental drinks.

Consolidate non-essential purchases to fewer days.

Adding buydays to your calendar (1) prevents [impulse purchases](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Impulse_purchase), (2) reduces [decision fatigue](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Decision_fatigue), and (3) improves [planning/budgeting](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Batch_processing).

[Paydays](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/payday) naturally complement buydays. To avoid a false sense of abundance, consider alternating payday and buyday weeks. For example, if your employer pays you on even-numbered Fridays, you could observe "buyday Fridays" on odd-numbered Fridays.

Tips
----------

* Block Amazon and other money pits via [parental self-controls](https://taylor.town/parental-self-controls).
* Most purchases are impulse purchases. If you have enough savings to avoid urgent decision-making, do so.
* Queue items on your [/wish page](https://taylor.town/wish-manifesto). Publishing your desires makes gifting less wasteful.
* Volunteer in your community on buydays. Engaging your humanity makes superfluous spending less attractive.

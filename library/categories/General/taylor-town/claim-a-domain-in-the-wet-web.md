The Company Formerly Known As Facebook usurped [a really cool word](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Metaverse).

In its stead, I offer "the wet web".

The wet web is the inevitable tangle of virtual layers imprinted onto our planet.

Gas stations are dry. Nightclubs are dry. Laundromats are dry.

Call of Duty is dry. VRChat is dry. Pinterest is dry.

Pokemon Go is wet. Strava is wet. Yelp is wet. [Mario Kart Live](https://mklive.nintendo.com) is wet.

---

AR devices will force existing apps to become wet:

* mobile games (e.g. Pokemon Go)
* social networks
* shopping
* professional tools
* media streaming
* messaging/chat
* search

Most apps will have to figure out how to straddle navigation of the wet and dry web simultaneously, similar to today's "responsive" design of desktop and mobile.

---

To claim your domain in the wet web:

* enhance hyperlocal search
* make a dry environment easier to navigate with wet apps
* publicize invisible stats
* identify a context for a [multimode extension](https://taylor.town/ar-modes)
* improve a narrow experience with a [monomode app](https://taylor.town/ar-modes)
* gamify chores
* make a dry activity easier with wet apps
* provide superhuman object recognition
* make a dry object more intuitive with wet apps

---

The wet web offers serious privacy risks. We must make the wet web [interoperable on our own terms](https://taylor.town/ar-interop).

Do you want Facebook strapped to your face?

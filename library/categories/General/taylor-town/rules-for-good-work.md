1. Never do anything non-obvious. Ask questions exhaustively.
2. Never do anything clever. Use modest methods and humble tools.
3. Never do anything difficult. Reject resistance. Enjoy low-hanging fruit.
4. Never do anything fancy. Spurn style.
5. Never do anything meaningless. Live your values.
6. Never do anything dull. Make fun.

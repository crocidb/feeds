1. Don't work hard.
----------

If you have waste and space, you're wasting space.

Transform scraps into food and foliage! Spend zero dollars and a few hours per year making plants.

Don't overthink it. Don't sink a bunch of time into it. Don't dive into research rabbit-holes and artificially sate your curiosity.

Failed experiments beat daydreams.

2. Make space.
----------

You can grow plants in a frontyard, balcony, window, garage, alley, etc.

If space is limited, ask friends and family [to donate space in exchange for produce](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sharecropping).

3. Reuse containers.
----------

Cardboard and plastics are valuable materials. Don't give them away for free!

* Use clear plastic bottles and containers [to root cuttings in water](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cutting_(plant)).
* Use large cardboard boxes and garbage-bag-liners as makeshift garden beds or drip trays.
* Poke holes in the bottoms of other plastic containers and set in larger drip trays.
* [Grow potatoes in cardboard boxes.](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=qUGXSetN5wc)
* Use medium-sized plastic bags for mushrooms and larger plants.
* Consider shredded paper or cardboard [as a mushroom substrate](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=qt4rO3KxW_k).

4. Save seeds.
----------

All fruits come with baby bonus plants inside. Vegetables regrow themselves from severed organs. Plants are weird.

* [Save seeds.](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Seed_saving)
* Barter yardwork services [on Craigslist](https://craigslist.org) for seeds and cuttings.
* [Revive kitchen scraps](https://www.urbancultivator.net/regrow-vegetable-cuttings) -- even [mushrooms](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VjxcV6O_WyU).
* [Reuse bouquets.](https://www.hunker.com/13426382/how-to-regrow-already-cut-flowers)
* Obtain cuttings via friends, neighbors, or [piracy](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Proplifting).

5. Collect compost.
----------

Soil is brown gold. Hoard organic material and [let it rot](https://www.amazon.com/Let-Rot-Composting-Down-Earth/dp/1580170234/ref=sr_1_1).

* You can compost [with garbage bags](https://www.wanderinghoofranch.com/compost-in-a-black-garbage-bag/) or use a [fancy tumbler](https://www.amazon.com/s?k=compost+tumbler).
* Ask neighbors if you can collect their compost on a weekly basis.
* [Become a worm rancher!](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=CvCCuvi6eHw)
* Borrow a friend's truck to get free dirt [on Craigslist](https://craigslist.org).

6. Forget watering.
----------

Plants combine carbon dioxide and water to make glucose. Plants use glucose as both fuel and building materials.

* Use [the Kratky method (hydroponic)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=KWUirDxgavc) to grow plants in a bucket without soil.
* Normalize neglect! It's okay to leave plants outside and pray for rain.
* You can connect a drip system to a [backyard rainwater resevoir](https://www.tank-depot.com/rainwater-tanks/).
* Strategically place pots next to existing sprinklers.
* Put [a drip resevoir](https://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B09SBG1KJ3/) next to your kitchen sink.
* Make [a toilet garden](https://www.instructables.com/Toilet-Garden/).

7. Share.
----------

With just one plant, you can clone an army of plants!

* [Plant legal plants in illegal places.](http://www.guerrillagardening.org)
* [Plant illegal plants in legal places.](https://www.complex.com/pop-culture/a/lewis-scrafton/elderly-woman-grew-huge-cannibas-plant-bbc)

Outside of theft and burglary, the only way to make money is to convince other people to give you their money.

### Honest Ways ###

There are two honest ways of convincing people to give you their money:

* make useful things
* perform useful services

### Dishonest Ways ###

There are also dishonest ways of convincing people to give you their money:

* make useless things and swindle people to pay for it
* perform useless services and swindle people to pay for it

---

Be Useful
----------

You and your creations can be useful in some of the following ways:

* treat and prevent pain
* produce sustainable food, clothing, shelter, transportation, manufacturing, materials, and energy
* enhance collective human wisdom
* promote peace
* distribute knowledge and wisdom
* allocate talent/capital for human/animal/plant flourishing
* fill hearts with inspiration, fear, laughter, anger, melancholy, etc.
* create tools for creators
* support useful people

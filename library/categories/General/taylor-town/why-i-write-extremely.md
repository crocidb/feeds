* Imperative statements are easy to read.
* Beliefs can be [useful and not true](https://sive.rs/run).
* Oversimplification is good.
* [Extrapolating from examples](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Few-shot_learning) is easier than alternatives.
* Extremes are fun.
* My readers have big brains with small attention spans.
* Of course everything has nuance.
* [Clown cars](https://taylor.town/clown-car) are memorable.
* Exaggeration is the most powerful tool ever crafted.

### Books ###

* ★★★★★ *A Confederacy of Dunces* by John Kennedy Toole :: Nutty Professor meets Don Quixote meets Infinite Jest. A literary landmark ahead of its time.
* ★★☆☆☆ *Bridget Jones's Diary* by Helen Fielding :: An endearing read about the difficulties of young adult life and womanhood in the modern era. Contains great tongue-in-cheek commentaries on health, feminism, egotism, and addiction.
* ★★☆☆☆ *Promise of Blood* by Brian McClellan :: It's a charming adventure/fantasy story with good characters and cool magic -- nothing revolutionary.

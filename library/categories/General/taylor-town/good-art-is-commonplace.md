Don't waste nuance on garbage. Use [RateYourMusic](https://rateyourmusic.com), [GoodReads](https://www.goodreads.com), [IMDB](https://www.imdb.com), and [Anilist](https://anilist.co) to skip flops.

Media mavens catalog and critique every form of art. Recommendation engines are excellent. Nobody needs to wade through waste involuntarily.

I rate [podcasts](https://taylor.town/podcasts), [books](https://taylor.town/books), and [music](https://taylor.town/music) with the following scale:

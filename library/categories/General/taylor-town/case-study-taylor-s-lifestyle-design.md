From a blank slate, write down everything you need and want.

Define minimalism for yourself. Fill your life with [delights](https://taylor.town/delights).

---

1. My Processes
----------

I am finite.

But I can carefully spend my remaining attention.

### Live/Survive ###

Fortune smiles upon me. At 30 years old, my "non-negotiables" eat half of my waking day:

>
>
> potty, sleep, parent, hydrate, eat, earn, exercise, clean, chores
>
>

### Thrive ###

Beyond necessities, I pursue [delights](https://taylor.town/delights):

1. [laughing with loved ones](https://taylor.town/delights#laughing)
2. [overcoming challenges](https://taylor.town/delights#challenges)
3. [exercising creativity](https://taylor.town/delights#creativity)
4. [inciting coincidence](https://taylor.town/delights#coincidence)
5. [helping people](https://taylor.town/delights#helping)
6. [experiencing novelty](https://taylor.town/delights#novelty)
7. [meeting cool people](https://taylor.town/delights#meeting)
8. [dancing](https://taylor.town/delights#dancing)

To spend as much time as possible balancing wants and needs, I maintain a daily routine:

1. check my daily calendar
2. organize task lists and "tickle" ideas
3. respond to messages and engage with communities
4. complete chores
5. tidy my home
6. exercise
7. [progress current project](https://taylor.town/now)
8. [write](https://taylor.town/)
9. [read](https://taylor.town/books)
10. commune with loved ones

2. My Consumables
----------

Temporary commodities fuel my ongoing processes:

>
>
> firewood, food, car fuel, heating/cooling electricity, cooking electricity, device electricty, toilet paper, toothpaste, toothbrush, mouthwash, floss, deodorant, shaving cream, blades, medications, home repair materials, soap, socializing trinkets, home cleaning supplies, events & travel costs, hazard & home insurance, prime, car insurance, cellular service, internet service, seeds
>
>

3. My Possessions
----------

I like listing my "whys" and "whats" together. My love deepens for the delightful objects around me. I surrender my cruft when I study what matters.

To align myself with my processes, I note my valuable durable goods:

### Necessities ###

* **shelter:** house, tools
* **sleep:** bed, sheets, pillow, winter blanket
* **eating:** fridge, cooking/heat appliance, food storage containers, eating utensils, bowls, cups
* **food production:** lights, racks, pots
* **sanitation:** shaving tools, bidet
* **transportation:** car
* **clothing:** socks, underwear, pants, t-shirts, jackets, bags
* **fitness:** weights, indoor bike
* **commerce:** credit/debit cards
* **verification:** IDs, passport, certificates

### Delights ###

* **comfort:** seating, lighting
* **convenience/comms:** smartphone, smartwatch
* **writing/publishing/programming:** computer, accessories
* **music listening:** headphones, speakers
* **beauty:** art, plants
* **reflection:** shrine, books, memorabilia
* **fashion:** "statement" clothes and accessories

I think it’s an exceptional book. It’s the reference I wish I had earlier in my life: an accessible, in-depth exploration of how circuits really work, from the motion of electrons to the dark art of embedded system programming.

The book is meant for the inquiring hobbyist, including those who have tried to learn the craft and hit a brick wall. With 420+ pages and 290+ meticulously-crafted color illustrations, *The Secret Life of Circuits* is there to help you bring your ideas to life.

The book is 100% complete but still going through layout, indexing, and proofreading. You can check out a sample chapter [here](https://lcamtuf.coredump.cx/electronics/preview).

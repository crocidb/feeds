So I returned to [Faster than Light](http://www.ftlgame.com/) again this weekend, this time for real. I bought it when it came out and I played it only in passing, but this weekend I played it a *ton*.

![](/images/ftl_start.png) Aaahh a new beginning in FTL. Will it be death once more

I generally don’t like games where you have to restart after you die, I have a tendency to get really upset when things go badly. Yes I do rage quit when I play cs. But I really liked [FTL](http://www.ftlgame.com/) for some reason. It is frustrating sure, especially when you’re facing the final boss once more, and once *again* you fail to beat him. I think I played 20 rounds from Friday to Sunday and yet I couldn’t beat him.

Until now.

I beat him using [The Torus](http://ftl.wikia.com/wiki/Ships#Engi_Cruiser), with two scrap recovery augments and a drone recovery arm early on. Needless to say I went full blown drones and I had some nice shielding and cloaking going on, but it was still a hard fight. The feeling after was so satisfying! I practically jumped out of my chair in pure happiness and I messaged my friends and Veronica “I beat him! I beat him!”.

Since then (since yesterday) I actually beat him again, but this time without the big yahoo reaction I was hoping for. But… I’m still plaing on easy mode… Do I dare to try normal next time?

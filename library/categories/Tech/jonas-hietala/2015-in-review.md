2015 Geek Achievements
==========

1. Finished my university studies and got a Master in Computer Science.
2. Discovered a new boardgame love: [Android Netrunner](https://boardgamegeek.com/boardgame/124742/android-netrunner).
3. Finished top 4 in my first Netrunner Store Championship.
4. Won a [small local Netrunner tournament](/blog/2015/09/18/netrunner_summer_tournament_linkping/).
5. Completed my [master thesis](/masters_thesis/) about recommender systems.
6. Participated in the 48 hour game making competition [Ludum Dare 33](http://ludumdare.com/compo/) with [Groar](/blog/2015/08/23/xgroar/).
7. Actually styled [this website](https://github.com/treeman/jonashietala) a little bit.
8. Achieved 12-kyu at [online-go](https://online-go.com/play) in [Go](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Go_(game)).

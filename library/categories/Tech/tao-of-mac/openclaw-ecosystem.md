OpenClaw is a self-hosted personal AI assistant you run on your own devices, with a gateway control plane that connects to the chat channels you already use (WhatsApp, Telegram, Slack, Discord, Google Chat, Signal, iMessage, Teams, WebChat, plus optional BlueBubbles, Matrix, and Zalo) and adds voice on macOS/iOS/Android alongside a live, controllable canvas. It ships with a CLI onboarding wizard (`openclaw onboard`) for macOS, Linux, and WSL2, with installs via npm/pnpm/bun. See the [OpenClaw site](https://openclaw.ai?utm_source=taoofmac.com&utm_medium=web&utm_campaign=unsolicited_traffic&utm_content=external_link) and [docs](https://docs.openclaw.ai?utm_source=taoofmac.com&utm_medium=web&utm_campaign=unsolicited_traffic&utm_content=external_link) for the canonical reference.

OpenClaw was created by [Peter Steinberger](https://steipete.me?utm_source=taoofmac.com&utm_medium=web&utm_campaign=unsolicited_traffic&utm_content=external_link). In his February 2026 post “OpenClaw, OpenAI and the future,” he described OpenClaw as a playground project that “created such waves,” and announced he is joining OpenAI to work on bringing agents to everyone while OpenClaw moves to a foundation and stays open and independent.<sup id="fnref:openai-note"><a class="footnote-ref" rel="anchor" href="/space/ai/agentic/openclaw#fn:openai-note" style="color: #0000cc;">1</a></sup>

This page tracks the core project and the variants that build on or integrate with its workflows within the wider [Agentic Systems](/space/ai/agentic) space.

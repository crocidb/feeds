Book: Louder Than Words: The New Science of How the Mind Makes Meaning by Benjamin K. Bergen. Rating: ⭐️⭐️⭐️. A good book, but maybe it’s not for me. I really like books that have a lot of research behind it, this is one of them. For almost every assumption made, the author reveals research done to support his point. This book made me realize that our words do not give meaning to something.

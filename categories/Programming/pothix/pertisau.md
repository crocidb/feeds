The Doist retreat 2022 happened in Pertisau, so I had the chance to visit Austria for the first time 🇦🇹! I really enjoyed the city. We got there in summer, and I heard it’s a totally different town in the winter. It’s quite cool to see how they adapt sports for the season. As an example of things I saw: they have Ski on winter, lake swimming and paragliding on summer.

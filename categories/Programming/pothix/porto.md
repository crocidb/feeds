I’ve been in Porto for the first time in 2017 when I first joined Doist, but also had the chance to visit the city in 2019 in the company retreat. I really like the city. Unlike Lisbon, it feels like a city that is not so big but is also not so small. To me, it feels right in the middle of a big and small city, with historical places and nice views.

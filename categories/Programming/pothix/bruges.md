In 2017 I had the chance to speak at FOSDEM in Brussels, and I used this opportunity to travel around Belgium a bit, one city every day. One of the cities I visited was Bruges. Everyone I spoke recommended me to visit Bruges, so I decided to go. It was not the city I liked the most, but it’s quite nice indeed. A very small city that still looks very medieval.

Book: The Phoenix Project: A Novel About IT, DevOps, and Helping Your Business Win by Gene Kim. Rating: ⭐️⭐️⭐️⭐️. This is a great book for IT folks. In case you worked (or still work) in a big company with lots of ITIL things that holds the development, this book will be like home for you. It is a typical hero’s journey and the characters are not so well developed (IMO) but the story is still good to follow.

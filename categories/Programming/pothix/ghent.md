In 2017 I had the chance to speak at FOSDEM in Brussels, and I used this opportunity to travel around Belgium a bit, one city every day. One of the cities I visited was Ghent. Ghent is certainly the city I enjoyed visiting the most in this whole trip. The city itself is quite nice and small and there a nice castle in the middle of it. It is worth mentioning that it was in Ghent that I saw snow for the first time in my whole life.

A while ago I was thinking about making something nice for my wife as a present for the Valentine’s Day. My eye caught a rather cool-looking 7-color eInk display with Raspberry Pico W on board - [Pimoroni Inky Frame](https://shop.pimoroni.com/products/inky-frame-5-7?variant=40048398958675). From the first glance it looked quite polished with ready-to-use SDKs in Python and C++. I thought it would be interesting to make a dynamic family photo frame out of it, something that my wife could have on her table, with ability to switch photos from time to time with a press of a button.

Hey everyone!

This is the list of all the changes we've done to our projects during the month of February.

![little ninj, a plus ninja, is riding a wave made up of rabbits while shouting: 9 years!](../media/content/videos/february_2025.jpg)

Summary Of Changes
----------

* **100r.ca**, added [Dinghy gelcoat](https://100r.ca/site/teapot_gelcoat.html), [Week 10](https://100r.ca/site/victoria_to_sitka_logbook.html#week10), [Week 11](https://100r.ca/site/victoria_to_sitka_logbook.html#week11), and [Week 12](https://100r.ca/site/victoria_to_sitka_logbook.html#week12) of the [Victoria to Sitka logbook](https://100r.ca/site/victoria_to_sitka.html). Updated [solar](https://100r.ca/site/solar.html) with new pictures and corrected information (this page used to be called solar tips).
* **[Nebu](https://wiki.xxiivv.com/site/nebu)**, released a spreadsheet editor.
* **[Grimgrains](https://100r.ca/site/grimgrains.html)**, added a new recipe: [Stovetop zaatar pizza](https://grimgrains.com/site/stovetop_zaatar_pizza.html).
* **[Store](https://100r.ca/site/store.html)**, added maritime [flag stickers](https://100r.ca/site/flag_stickers.html) for sale.
* **[Rabbit Waves](https://100r.ca/site/rabbit_waves.html)**, added a new page: [Emergency Bag](https://rabbitwaves.ca/site/emergency_bag.html). Updated [Morse Code with Flags](https://rabbitwaves.ca/site/morse_flags.html) page with animations, released a printable [zine](https://rabbitwaves.ca/media/zines/communication.png)(see [how to fold](https://kokorobot.ca/site/folding_zine.html) a zine).

On February 14th, we celebrated our 9th year living aboard our beloved [Pino](https://100r.ca/site/pino.html). Read a [short text](https://wiki.xxiivv.com/site/fitness) by Devine, which expands on what it means to truly be a generalist.

Despite the weather being less-than-ideal, we were able to [install](https://rabbits.srht.site/days/2025/02/20.html) our replacement solar panels, and revisit our notes on [solar installations](https://100r.ca/site/solar.html).

Devine completed [Nebu](https://wiki.xxiivv.com/site/nebu), a spritesheet editor as well as a [desktop calendar](https://wiki.xxiivv.com/media/generic/calendar.png), alongside many other little [desktop utilities](https://wiki.xxiivv.com/site/utilities.html). Nebu is just over 8.3 kB, a bit less than a blank excel file.

In times of increasing climate and political instability, it is a good time to get together with your community and make plans for emergencies. Consider reading [Tokyo Bosai](https://archive.org/details/disaster-preparedness-tokyo) about disaster preparedness, this elaborate document deals with disasters that occur specifically in Japan, but many of the recommendations are useful regardless. We released a new page on rabbit waves with suggestions on what to pack in an [Emergency Bag](https://rabbitwaves.ca/site/emergency_bag.html). Remember, every emergency bag is different, and what is essential varies per person.

We also put together a print-it-yourself zine, which combines useful information about [Morse Code and ](https://rabbitwaves.ca/site/morse.html)[Signal Flags](https://rabbitwaves.ca/site/flags_maritime.html). If you have printed the zine and don't know how to fold it, see Rek's [illustrated instructions](https://kokorobot.ca/site/folding_zine.html). Speaking of signal flags, we printed [stickers](https://100r.ca/site/flag_stickers.html) of Rek's ICS flag drawings.

The nice weather finally arrived this week and we were able to redo Teapot's [gelcoat](https://100r.ca/site/teapot_gelcoat.html). This was our first time working with gelcoat, our friends Rik & Kay, who lent us their workspace, were very patient and generous teachers. We will continue the project later when the gelcoat has cured.

**Book Club:** This month we are reading *The Goldfinch* by Donna Tartt.

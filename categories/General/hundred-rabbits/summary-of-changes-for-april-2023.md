Hey everyone! This is the list of all the changes we've done to our projects and apps during the month of April. We'll also be reporting in our on position in the world, and on our future plans.

Summary Of Changes
----------

* **100r.ca**, added [charging electronics](https://100r.ca/site/charging_electronics), [solar cooking](https://100r.ca/site/solar_cooking.html) and [solar evacuated tube cooking](https://100r.ca/site/solar_evacuated_tube_cooking.html), and updated [insulation](https://100r.ca/site/insulation.html#apr2023), [thruhulls](https://100r.ca/site/thruhulls.html#apr2023).
* **[Oquonie](https://100r.ca/site/oquonie.html)**, released the game for Uxn. We have a [Windows95+ compatible version](https://merveilles.town/@voxel/110189746091696167)[Mastodon video].
* **[Left](https://100r.ca/site/left.html)**, various UX improvements, including negative length selection.
* **Note Pad**, ported the [classic macintosh Note Pad](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Fp6io-G6dQQ) application to Varvara, download it [here](https://git.sr.ht/~rabbits/uxn-utils/tree/main/item/gui/notepad/notepad.tal).
* **[Turye](https://wiki.xxiivv.com/site/turye.html)**, total [redesign](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=aRa7fI53w_0).
* **[Beetbug](https://wiki.xxiivv.com/site/beetbug.html)**, added support for [comments](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=vd3bcRCjNVg) in symbol files.

[Oquonie](https://100r.ca/site/oquonie.html) is out and it is playable! A big thank you to those who helped us test the game on a variety of devices and systems all the way to Windows95. This version of Oquonie differs a little from the original, in that some of the puzzles and secrets have changed. We hope that those who played the original will too enjoy this one. We have a special build of the game on [itchio](https://hundredrabbits.itch.io/oquonie) with the emulator and rom combined, if you have a false-negative virus warning on Windows 10, you will need to use the standard uxn32 emulator and rom.

We have watched as people implemented [their own emulators](https://github.com/hundredrabbits/awesome-uxn) and were able to play Oquonie on a Varvara of their own making. The current [implementation documentation](http://wiki.xxiivv.com/site/varvara.html) might need to be improved, if you have feedback for things that could be clearer, please let us know!

We published our experiments with [solar cooking](https://100r.ca/site/solar_cooking.html) this month, and wrote a more detailed post on [solar evacuated tube cooking](https://100r.ca/site/solar_evacuated_tube_cooking.html). The real test will happen this summer. We plan to keep a log of everything we cook, how long it takes, and the conditions(sun, overcast, temp etc).

We're still closing a few projects aboard Pino, to get it ready for some summer sailing. Our plans for now are loose, we're hauling the boat out of the water in early June, with plans to sail back towards Desolation Sound afterward, with a possible stop by Jervis Inlet.

### News ###

Pino sailed a bit more northward this month and went all the way to the [Octopus Islands](https://100r.ca/site/octopus_islands.html). We visited this place during a very windy week, with winds blowing 30-35 kts the entire time. We got acquainted with a few northern rapids, like Hole in the Wall and Surge Narrows. It is a bit of a mindfuck to think that in these waters the tide ebbs north and floods south.

This summer has been especially arid, and because of it the province has seen a lot forest fires (see pictures of [our smokey transit](https://100r.ca/site/sturt_bay.html#aug2023)). Trails that we know and love on Cortes Island, that are usually wet and muddy, were bone dry this year. We had a few days of hard rain, during that time we collect [rain](https://100r.ca/site/rain.html) water and go for walks to look for [slugs and snails](https://100r.ca/site/hathayim_marine_park.html#jul2023).

Both of us have been drawing a lot this month, see this [Neoneve portrait](https://merveilles.town/@neauoire/110982150120018215)(Mastodon) by Devine (drawn with [Oekaki](https://git.sr.ht/~rabbits/oekaki)), and this [sketch thread](https://merveilles.town/@rek/110934243447981216)(Mastodon) by Rek..

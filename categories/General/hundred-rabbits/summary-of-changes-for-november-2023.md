Hey everyone!

This is the list of all the changes we've done to our projects during the month of November.

Summary Of Changes
----------

* **[Wiktopher](https://100r.ca/site/wiktopher.html)**, drew some [new art](https://merveilles.town/@rek/111342263287492914)(Mastodon), finalized a [few translations](https://merveilles.town/@rek/111343213701036541)(Mastodon), and released it the project on [Itchio](https://hundredrabbits.itch.io/wiktopher-desert-tales)!
* **[Thousand Rooms](https://100r.ca/site/thousand_rooms.html)**, [translated the book](https://merveilles.town/@neauoire/111427947623485478)(Mastodon) into Solresol.
* **100r.ca**, added a still map of our Pacific circumnavigation in [where](https://100r.ca/site/where.html), added [repair](https://100r.ca/site/repair.html), [effect of combining dissimilar metals](https://100r.ca/site/effect_of_combining_dissimilar_metals.html), [bikes on a boat](https://100r.ca/site/bikes_on_a_boat.html), [upholstery](https://100r.ca/site/upholstery.html) and updated [lifelines](https://100r.ca/site/lifelines.html).
* **[Busy Doing Nothing](https://100r.ca/site/busy_doing_nothing.html)**, added [GPS coordinates](https://merveilles.town/@rek/111365215195099215)(Mastodon) for every listed day of travel.
* **[Hakum](https://kokorobot.ca/site/hakum.html)**, designed handwritten font for the comic and [wrote a post](https://kokorobot.ca/site/making_a_font.html) about the process.
* **[Elmet Brae](https://beldamrecords.bandcamp.com/album/elmet-brae-the-land)**, released a collaborative album with members of the Merveilles community and friends. Made the album cover in collaboration with [Rostiger](https://nchrs.xyz), who also made the [Varvara Zine](https://wiki.xxiivv.com/site/varvara_zine.html).
* **[Solrela](https://rabbits.srht.site/solrela/)**, released a Solresol translator website to replace the now defunct Sidosi translator.

### News ###

[Wiktopher](https://100r.ca/site/wiktopher.html) is finally finished. We started this book in 2017 while in [French Polynesia](https://100r.ca/site/french_polynesia.html), constrained by power limitations and hardware failures. Writing demanded less energy than drawing digitally, Rek could use the Chromebook to work (see [tools ecosystem](https://100r.ca/site/tools_ecosystem.html)). You can read the first two chapters of the finished story **[here](https://100r.ca/media/content/projects/wiktopher_preview.pdf)[4.1 MiB]** as a PDF. If you liked what you've read, we hope you'll consider [reading the rest](https://hundredrabbits.itch.io/wiktopher-desert-tales)!

We treated ourselves this month to new saloon cushions(see [upholstery](https://100r.ca/site/upholstery.html)). We sleep and live on these, it's so nice to have plump cushions again!

**IMPORTANT**. *You may have heard but the service we use to send our newsletter(TinyLetter) is shutting down early next year (Feb 2024). We are in the process of setting up an alternative, we'll provide details on that in December's newsletter and on this website.*

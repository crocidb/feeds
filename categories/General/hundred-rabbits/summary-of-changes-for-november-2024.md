Hey everyone!

This is the list of all the changes we've done to our projects during the month of November.

![6 algae-eared rabbits posing and playing together](../media/content/videos/november_2024.jpg)

Summary Of Changes
----------

* **100r.ca**, added an article named [A Shining Place Built Upon The Sand](https://100r.ca/site/shining_sand.html), and [Week 6](https://100r.ca/site/victoria_to_sitka_logbook.html#week6) to the [Victoria to Sitka Logbook](https://100r.ca/site/victoria_to_sitka_logbook.html).
* **[Rabbit Waves](https://100r.ca/site/rabbit_waves.html)**, added a page on [Morse Code](https://rabbitwaves.ca/site/morse.html) and on [Morse Code with flags](https://rabbitwaves.ca/site/morse_flags.html).
* **[Left](https://100r.ca/site/left.html)**, redesigned with a [bigger font](https://100r.ca/media/content/stream/2024_leftfont.jpg), for aging eyes.

Our website has a new look! The illustrated algae-eared rabbit nav helped solve the problem of navigating on mobile. We added a lot of information to this wiki over the years, creating separate portals for its evergrowing content was inevitable, we hope you like the re-design. Some of the content has shifted, and we've simplified many of the pages.

A couple of folks on [Merveilles](https://merveilles.town) got together recently and made a Diablo Tribute tape. A limited run of physical cassettes are currently in production, but in the meantime the tribute album is available to download on [Bandcamp](https://spectralepoch.bandcamp.com/album/a-tribute-to-diablo).

Next month on December 6th, Devine will share the stage with Iszoloscope, Oddie(Orphx) & Creature at Foufounes Electriques in Montréal as part of [AMP Industrial Events](https://ampindustrialevents.com/index.html). Then on the 7th, we will both(remotely) present a summary of all the interesting analog communication schemes that inspired and found their way into [Rabbit Waves](https://100r.ca/site/rabbit_waves.html) and [Wiktopher](https://100r.ca/site/wiktopher.html) for [Iterations 2024](https://creativecodingutrecht.nl/en/calendar/iterations-2024) organized by Creative Coding Utrecht.

Devine's talk for Handmade Seattle 2024 entitled A Shining Palace Built Upon the Sand was [released online](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=OfnEfFb8yks&t=1908s)(YouTube), we also released the [written transcript](https://100r.ca/site/shining_sand.html).

Due to the ongoing Canada Post strike we had to close the sale of stickers in our [store](https://100r.ca/site/store.html), we'll let you know once we resume operations (this also applies to Patreon supporters, we'll ship perks your way as soon as we can).

**Book Club:** This month we are still reading *The Memoirs of Sherlock Holmes* by Arthur Conan Doyle.

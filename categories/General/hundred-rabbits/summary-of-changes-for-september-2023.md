### News ###

Pino returned to the Victoria docks a little early this year so Devine could go to [Strange Loop 2023](https://thestrangeloop.com). We had a lovely sail that day, with clear skies, and 10 kts on the beam. Despite being a little food and sleep deprived, Devine's presentation went well, we will share the recording here once it is released.

Rek has been busy editing [Wiktopher](https://100r.ca/site/wiktopher.html), finessing the conlangs featured in the story. One of the featured languages is Ilken, a whistled language, designed for long-distance communication, and playable with instruments. A few years ago Devine designed a language for it, but we decided to instead use a modern variation of [Solresol](http://wiki.xxiivv.com/site/solresol.html), a musical language by Jean-François Sudre. Rek [drew a fanart of the mascot of Solresol](http://wiki.xxiivv.com/media/refs/mimi.jpg), and Devine a [communication lantern](http://wiki.xxiivv.com/media/diary/826.jpg).

Currently, Devine is working at translating [Thousand Rooms](https://100r.ca/site/thousand_rooms.html)(Famimi Remisolla) in Solresol as practice. We're also editing a Brazilian Portuguese version, to be released next month.

We've been toying with the idea of making an audiobook for the story, and asked [Paul B.](https://pbat.ch/) to use their voice synthesis tool [Gesture](https://pbat.ch/wiki/gesture/) to try and hear what [a poem in Ilken(Solresol)](https://merveilles.town/@patchlore/111042127852885392) sounds like. The result was so lovely that it made Rek cry of joy.

Rek's [sketch thread](https://merveilles.town/@rek/110934243447981216)(Mastodon) is still going. Devine will be producing a lot more art next month for Drawtober!

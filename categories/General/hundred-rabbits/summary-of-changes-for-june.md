### News ###

Pino is currently in Desolation Sound, enjoying the quiet. We've written a few posts about some of the anchorages we've visited, like [melanie cove](https://100r.ca/site/melanie_cove.html), [grace harbour](https://100r.ca/site/grace_harbour.html) and [hathayim marine park](https://100r.ca/site/hathayim_marine_park.html). We're updating [our track](https://100r.ca/site/about_us.html#where) as we go.

Devine has been adding a lot of notes to the [permacomputing wiki](https://permacomputing.net) this month. We also started a little arcade game named Inle, something that we could play together on the boat in the style of Gunbound/Tank Wars... but with rabbits (we finished reading Watership Down last month, so we blame that, haha). We don't have a project page yet for it, but we shared some images on the post for [grace harbour](https://100r.ca/site/grace_harbour.html), where we started working on the game.

Rek produced a video about [image optimization](https://youtu.be/cfrB4Gvpj8c) for the Photographer's Gallery (as part of their [Green Hacks](https://thephotographersgallery.org.uk/green-hacks) video series) last month.

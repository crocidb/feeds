Hey everyone!

This is the list of all the changes we've done to our projects during the month of November.

<img src="../media/content/videos/november_2025.jpg" alt="2 rabbits with birthday hats cuddling up next to each other, confetti is in the air" loading="lazy" width="1200" height="679">

* **[Thousand Rooms](../site/thousand_rooms.html)**, added an English version with one type of [dyslexia-friendly typeface](https://hundredrabbits.itch.io/thousand-rooms/devlog/1129255/opendyslexic-thousand-rooms). A Danish version is also on the way.
* **[Orca](../site/orca.html)**, improved the interface, released an [Orca Cheatsheet](../media/content/projects/zine_orca.png) for an upcoming workshop, and increased the parity between Orca versions with new tests.
* **[Drifblim](https://wiki.xxiivv.com/site/drifblim.html)**, implemented a [tug-o-war](https://merveilles.town/@neauoire/115614176516283188)(Mastodon) memory model.
* **[Punk Rabbits](https://kokorobot.ca/site/punk_rabbits.html)**, completed 8 new rabbits(#57-64).
* **[Uxn](../site/uxn.html)**, wrote a standalone [Uxn REPL](https://wiki.xxiivv.com/etc/uxnbot.tal.txt), and improved [Uxn5](https://rabbits.srht.site/uxn5/).
* **[M291](https://wiki.xxiivv.com/site/m291.html)**, fixed an annoying crashing bug.

This month, we celebrate Hundred Rabbit's 10 year anniversary and remember with great fondness how, in November 2015, we sat together at a coffee shop in Montreal, puzzling over how we would start a new life on the water. Here's hoping for 10 more years of learning and documenting experiments around low-tech and resilience!

We finally stashed our summer sailing gear, we compressed everything into vacuum bags drawing out as much air as we could to discourage mold. We're entering the month of December in Victoria, and we are beginning to feel the increasing dampness of winter in our clothes, we also see it as the condensation gathers on Pino's windows.

Devine spent the last few days in Austria for the [Ultramateria Festival](http://www.ultramateria.art/) talking with local artists and activists about the design [philosophy](../site/philosophy.html) of Hundred Rabbits. Devine also gave an [Orca](../site/orca.html) [workshop](https://merveilles.town/@neauoire/115632512828333614), played some techno in a [gorgeous venue](https://wiki.xxiivv.com/site/lives.html#895), made a brief appearance on [Austrian television](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_iGkp1Qat0c) and [Fireside Fedi](https://video.firesidefedi.live/w/3HkP3xFw82fbGXcbj99oZh).

Rek spent time [troubleshooting](https://merveilles.town/@rek/115623105983817106) a raw water leak in Calcifer II, gaining in-depth knowledge of yet another part of the engine. What was learned was documented in the ever-growing [engine care](../site/engine_care.html) portal under [raw water pump](../site/engine_care.html#saltwaterpump). Rek has also been working on the upcoming [Playdate version](https://rabbits.srht.site/days/2025/12/02.html) of [Donsol](../site/donsol.html)!

**Book Club:** This month we are still reading *Madame Bovary: Provincial Manners* by Gustave Flaubert.

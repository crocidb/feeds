Hey everyone! This is the list of all the changes we've done to our projects and apps during the month of November. We'll also be reporting in our on position in the world, and on our future plans.

Summary Of Changes
----------

* **[GrimGrains](https://grimgrains.com)**, added a [quick grilled cheese](http://grimgrains.com/site/quick_grilled_cheese.html) recipe.
* **[Noodle](https://100r.ca/site/noodle.html)**, updated the guide, and added [app icon](https://merveilles.town/@neauoire/109280995419859430)(Mastodon) to the about modal.
* **[Nasu](https://100r.ca/site/nasu.html)**, added [app icon](https://merveilles.town/@neauoire/109282043310182229)(Mastodon) to the about modal.
* **[Store](https://100r.ca/site/store.html)**, added sheets of [Ecosystem Rabbit Stickers(gen2)](https://100r.ca/site/store.html#stickers2) to the store.
* **[Uxntal](https://wiki.xxiivv.com/site/uxntal.html)**, created a list of Uxntal examples with equivalent C sources, you can find them [here](https://git.sr.ht/~rabbits/uxntal-examples).
* **[Wiktopher](https://100r.ca/site/wiktopher.html)**, made a progress bar (50% in the correction process) on the project page. We have finished processing chapter 5, and are currently applying corrections to chapter 6 & 7.
* **[Donsol](https://100r.ca/site/donsol.html)**, updated GBA rom with a few fixes.
* **Soroban**, made a little [desktop graphical abacus](https://merveilles.town/web/@neauoire/109417640512376459)(Mastodon).
* **[Uxn](https://100r.ca/site/uxn.html)**, ported to [DuskOS](https://git.sr.ht/~vdupras/duskos)(SourceHut).
* **[Dexe](https://wiki.xxiivv.com/site/dexe.html)**, redesigned and [much faster](https://git.sr.ht/~rabbits/dexe)(SourceHut).

We put some [red lights](https://100r.ca/media/content/stream/2022_11xmas.jpg) on Pino, to help combat winter gloom. We also started growing another lion's mane mushroom on board(we grew the same variety last fall), it already has a little [tuft](https://100r.ca/media/content/stream/2022_11lionmanes2.jpg)!

Devine's talk **Weathering Software Winter** for Handmade Seattle was recorded, watch it [here](https://vimeo.com/771406693#t=85m30s)(Vimeo). The video is for the entire first day of the conference, so for your convenience Devine's talk starts at 1h25min.

A little while ago [Rostiger](https://nchrs.xyz/) drew an amazing [series of illustrations](https://nchrs.xyz/uxn_notes.html) explaining Uxn, and how it works. [Ben](https://mineralexistence.com/about.html) made a zine out of it and [gave us a copy](https://100r.ca/media/content/stream/uxnzine.jpg). Make your own Uxn zine using [this PDF](https://mineralexistence.com/files/uxn-zine.pdf)[1.38 MB].

![Before AI’s Kepler Moment - Are LLMs the Epicycles of Intelligence?](https://ashvardanian.com/llm-epicycles/LLM-Epicycles.jpg)

I’ve always been fascinated by AI and mega-projects — and as [I work on AI infrastructure](https://github.com/ashvardanian), you might assume I’m equally fascinated by the current LLM race.

In reality, I’m far more skeptical than most. While LLMs are undeniably useful, I’m not convinced “intelligence” is even the right scale to measure them against. The analogy I keep returning to comes not from computer science, but from astronomy: the story of *epicycles*.

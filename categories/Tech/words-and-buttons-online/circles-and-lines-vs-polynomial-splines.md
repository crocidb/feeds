Usually, when you want to make a parametric curve, you should go for a polynomial spline. But sometimes, for the reasons mentioned only in the second half of this page to keep you intrigued, you are not satisfied with polynomials. You have to look elsewhere. This page shows you one possible option apart from polynomials and it is an old-school parametric curve made from arcs and line segments stitched together.

2013 Geek Achievements
==========

1. Solved [213 UVa problems](http://uhunt.felix-halim.net/id/115705).
2. Got 43rd at [NWERC 2013](https://chipcie.ch.tudelft.nl/).
3. Placed high in [IMPA](http://www.ida.liu.se/projects/impa/new/results), with one turn won.
4. Completed the online course [Programming Languages](https://class.coursera.org/proglang-002/class) in Coursera.
5. Wrote some ML, Racket and Ruby.
6. Won the [TDDD56](http://www.ida.liu.se/~TDDD56/) Parallel Sorting Contest.
7. Got my first summer job as a programmer at Configura. Great fun, great place to work.
8. Wrote a lot of C and C++.
9. Switched my [site](https://github.com/treeman/jonashietala) generator from Jekyll to [Hakyll](http://jaspervdj.be/hakyll/) and wrote a little Haskell.
10. For the first time achieved full marks in an exam - TDDD20 Construction and Analysis of Algorithms.
11. Completed an extra math course: TATA64 Graph Theory. Fun course.
12. Finished my 3 first years in University, now I can choose my own courses!
13. Completed the compiler course in school and learned a bunch.
14. Unlocked all ships in [FTL](http://www.ftlgame.com/) and beat it a bunch of times. Fantastic game!

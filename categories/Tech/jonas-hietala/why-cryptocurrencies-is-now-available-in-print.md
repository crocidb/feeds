Today is a big milestone for me, as this marks a big milestone in the biggest personal project I’ve undertaken.

My book ‘[Why Cryptocurrencies?](https://whycryptocurrencies.com/)’ is released in print today! It’s my attempt to explain what cryptocurrencies are and what problems they solve, in a simple and approachable manner.

I’ve been working on the book in the open [on GitHub](https://github.com/treeman/why_cryptocurrencies) and [the first recorded commit](https://github.com/treeman/why_cryptocurrencies/commit/5478b8a) is from Dec 19, 2018, with a chapter outline. 1237 commits later, and a bunch of work outside that, and the book is now Done™. (Only a year after my [blog post saying it was done](/blog/2020/04/29/my_book_why_cryptocurrencies_is_done/)…)

If you want to check it out, you can [read it online for free](https://whycryptocurrencies.com/toc.html) or you can [buy](https://whycryptocurrencies.com/#print) it from wherever you buy your books.
